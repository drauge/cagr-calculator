Dutch Box 3 assumptions

Fiscal partner uses 2025 joint Box 3 allowance (EUR 115,368) and joint debt threshold (EUR 7,600), allocated fully to your side.

Box 3 debt allocation model

Box 3 debt allocation is now calculated inside every scenario year. The calculator uses that year's forecasted ETF value as Dutch investments, the forecasted 2nd-property position as foreign treaty-exempt real estate, and the remaining 2nd mortgage as Box 3 debt. See the yearly details table for exempt debt allocation, Dutch debt allocation, treaty relief, and final Box 3 tax.

2nd property local real estate tax

Generalized progressive real-estate tax. Use the official taxable / mass valuation value, not purchase price and not equity. Taxable value is divided by the allocation divisor first, then brackets are applied per allocated share. Example: if two spouses each own half, use divisor 2.

Bracket lower/upper limits are in EUR. Leave upper limit empty for no cap.

NL property & mortgage

Scenario-specific assumptions

A. Repay 2nd mortgage fully + buy NL property

Scenario A dynamically finds the earliest year when ETF + extra cash can fully repay the 2nd mortgage and pay NL purchase costs. 2nd-mortgage repayment and NL property purchase happen in that same year. Scenario A dynamic year now tests execution feasibility only; later mortgage affordability is shown separately via liquidity shortfall and pension disposable income.

B. Sell 2nd property + buy NL property

Scenario B execution year links sale year and NL purchase year. Change execution year to test delayed sale / delayed NL purchase.

Sale month is treated as after that month’s mortgage payment.

C. Keep 2nd mortgage until maturity + no NL property purchase

Baseline investment-compounding scenario. Useful even if it does not solve Dutch mortgage borrowing capacity.

D. Sell 2nd property + invest into ETF + no NL property purchase

Sale month is treated as after that month’s mortgage payment.

This tests selling the 2nd property and reallocating net proceeds into ETF without buying an NL property. Execution year lets you model a later sale before retirement.
